# Data Processing Addendum (Template)

Last updated: 2026-03-02

This DPA template is provided for adaptation by your legal team.

## 1. Parties

- Controller: [Your Company]
- Processor/Sub-processor: [CRM Provider / Hosting Provider]

## 2. Subject Matter

Processing of client contact data synchronized from WHMCS to external CRM via CRM Connector.

## 3. Duration

For the term of your CRM service agreement, unless earlier terminated.

## 4. Nature and Purpose

Customer relationship management, contact enrichment, sales follow-up, and customer support workflow continuity.

## 5. Categories of Data

- Identifiers (client ID)
- Contact details (name, email)
- Company metadata

## 6. Data Subjects

End customers, prospects, and organization contacts managed in WHMCS.

## 7. Security Measures

- Encryption in transit (TLS)
- Access controls and role-based permissions
- Audit logging and key rotation policies

## 8. Sub-processors

Controller must maintain and disclose an up-to-date sub-processor list where required.

## 9. International Transfers

Use SCCs or equivalent lawful transfer mechanisms when required.

## 10. Deletion/Return

Upon contract termination, processor must delete or return personal data per controller instruction, unless retention is legally required.

## 11. Audit Rights

Controller may request reasonable compliance evidence or third-party certifications.
